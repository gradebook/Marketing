Gradebook is a free tool designed to help keep track of your grades.

Functional

Gradebook was designed by students, for students. We focus on providing better insights into your progress so that you can focus on what's most important.

Consolidated

Gradebook is your one-stop shop to keep track of your grades in all your courses at any school on all your devices.

What makes Gradebook awesome

School is stressful, and tracking your grades shouldn't make it worse. Our mission is to provide a fast, simple, and beatiful way to track your progress and we constantly add features to support this.

Collect grades into categories

Collect grades, homeworks, labs, and quizzes into categories to accurately track your progress and completion.

Drag and drop grades

Reorder and arrange grades and categories to customize your class layout.

Compute fractional point values

Not all grades are out of 100. Enter a fractional amount to get an exact grade without doing math!
